Species Distribution

European Rabbit

The European rabbit is native to southwestern Europe and northwest Africa.

Cottontail

Hare

Hares are fast-running, long-eared mammals in the genus Lepus.

Jackrabbit

Jackrabbits are hares found in western North America.

Pygmy Rabbit

The pygmy rabbit is the smallest rabbit in North America, with adults weighing about 1 pound.

About This Dashboard

This Global Rabbit Population Dashboard provides comprehensive insights into rabbit populations worldwide, tracking trends, distribution patterns, and conservation statuses.

The data presented is collected from various wildlife organizations, research institutions, and conservation groups around the world.

This dashboard is designed to help researchers, conservationists, and wildlife managers make informed decisions about rabbit conservation and management.

Data Sources

  • World Wildlife Foundation (WWF)
  • International Union for Conservation of Nature (IUCN)
  • National wildlife databases from various countries
  • Academic research studies and publications
  • Citizen science initiatives and wildlife surveys

Technical Implementation

This dashboard is built with Python using Flask, Pandas, and Plotly Dash for interactive visualizations.
